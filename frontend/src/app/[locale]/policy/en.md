# Privacy Policy

The series of services provided by Rito Labeler Kit (hereinafter referred to as the "Service") are operated individually by the developer @usounds.work.  
The Service respects users’ privacy and handles personal information appropriately as described below.

## 1. Information Collected
The Service may collect the following information:
- DID (Decentralized Identifier) used in AT Protocol
- Bookmark schema information entered on this website

## 2. Purpose of Use
The collected information is used solely for the purpose of providing the labeling features offered by the Service.

## 3. Provision to Third Parties
The Service does not provide collected information to third parties on its own initiative.  
However, all information is stored in the PDS selected by the user. Please note that depending on the PDS configuration, such information may be accessible to third parties.

## 4. Use of External Services
The Service uses the following external services:
- PDS (for authentication and data storage)
- Google Analytics 4 (for access analysis)  
  → [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 5. Information Management
Label information is published to the PDS, and no specific retention period is defined.

## 6. User Rights
Information including DIDs is managed by users themselves and is not subject to deletion requests to the Service.  
Information stored in the PDS cannot be deleted by third parties. Therefore, if you wish to delete label information, please do so yourself using the features provided on this website.

## 7. Revisions
This Privacy Policy may be revised as necessary. Any revisions will be announced on this website.
